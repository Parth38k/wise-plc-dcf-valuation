# Wise PLC (WISE.L) — DCF Valuation Model

A 3-statement DCF model valuing Wise PLC, built as part of my 
investment portfolio alongside my MSc Investments at the University 
of Birmingham.

## Methodology
- 3 years historical financials (FY2023-FY2025)
- 5-year forecast (FY2026E-FY2030E)
- WACC: 8.47% (CAPM-based cost of equity, after-tax cost of debt)
- Terminal value via Gordon Growth perpetuity method
- Trading comparables benchmark against PayPal, Adyen, and Remitly

## Key Finding
Implied share price of £16.78 versus current market price of £10.87, 
implying 54.4% upside — driven primarily by margin expansion 
assumptions and a terminal growth rate of 3.0%.

## Notable Adjustment
Customer funds (~£14bn) were excluded from cash and capital structure 
calculations, since Wise holds these as a regulated e-money institution 
rather than as corporate assets — a common pitfall in fintech valuation.
